Welcome to Sahara Royal Gold Refinery

Sahara Royal Gold Refinery Limited is a privately owned Ghanaian company, valued at $7 million, specializing in the purchase, sale, assay, smelting, refining (to 99.99% purity), and export of gold and other precious minerals. Our state-of-the-art equipment was acquired from Italimpianti Orafi in Italy, ensuring world-class standards across our operations.

What We Do

At Sahara Royal Gold Refinery Limited, we uphold the highest standards of transparency and accountability, ensuring every ounce of gold is secure and meticulously documented. Our commitment is to guarantee that every client walks away with complete confidence and satisfaction.

Gold Trading Assay Smelting Refining Vault Storage Export Services

Gold Trading

At Sahara Royal Gold Refinery, we trade in both doré and bullion, buying and selling gold at competitive market prices. Through strict adherence to international standards, we ensure transparency, uncompromising quality, and the utmost trust in every transaction.

Assay

We conduct precise testing and analysis to determine the purity and composition of gold, utilizing state-of-the-art equipment. Our expertise encompasses both fire assay and water assay methods, ensuring the highest level of accuracy and reliability.

Smelting

Our team of professionals expertly heats and melts gold in all its forms—whether grains, nuggets, or bars—to effectively separate and eliminate impurities. This meticulous process ensures an accurate determination of the gold’s true content, upholding the highest standards of precision and integrity.

Refining

Sahara Royal Gold Refinery Limited utilizes cutting-edge techniques to refine gold to 99.99% purity, meeting the highest standards of the international gold market

Vault Storage

Secure, fully insured vault facilities offer unparalleled protection and safekeeping for precious metals. Equipped with advanced security systems, continuous surveillance, and stringent protocols, these facilities ensure assets remain safeguarded with utmost confidentiality, integrity, and care.

Export Services

Professional handling, meticulous documentation, and streamlined logistics ensure the seamless export and international shipment of gold. Every step — from secure packaging and compliance with regulatory requirements to coordination with trusted global carriers — is executed with precision and transparency, guaranteeing that clients’ precious assets reach their destinations safely, efficiently, and in full accordance with international trade standards.
