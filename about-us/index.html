About Sahara

Sahara Royal Gold Refinery is a multi-million dollar gold refinery, privately owned by a Ghanaian entrepreneur. With the capacity to refine up to 300 kilograms of gold dore into bullion of 99.95% to 99.99% purity each day, we stand at the forefront of Ghana’s precious metals industry. Our mission is to elevate Ghana’s presence on the global stage by exemplifying professionalism and delivering unparalleled export excellence to clients worldwide.
